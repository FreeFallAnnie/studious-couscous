Show Me (the Data)

Show Me (the Data) is about data and presentation. The subskills include data visualisation, modelling and simulation with agent-based modelling, including python and NetLogo, along with the addition of storytelling with data.

Subskills

  • A = Data Visualisation
  • B = Agent-Based Modelling
  • C = Data Storytelling

Data Visualisation (A)

The data visualisation skill did not eventuate in the way I foresaw. While I envisioned digital data display, a conversation with Tritian led to physical collection of data, and display, with colour-coded responses. I was happy with the way the data was shown; however, I would also like to explore further visualisation tools and create digital displays.

Agent-Based Modelling (B)

I have been doing the agent-based modelling course, offered by the Santa Fe Institute on their learning platform - Complexity Explorer. Along with researching agent-based modelling as a methodology, and having conversations with others, including Sungyeon.
