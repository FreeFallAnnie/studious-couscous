Tinkerer

Tinkerer is a mindset I made-up that can underpin any hands-on activity. The skill of tinkering, is about playing, experimenting, and working to get better by incrementally exploring – following my learning scale, it’s trying something others have done, mimic them, change it up, then see if you can work towards independence, and sharing what I’ve learnt.

Laser cutting (C)

I began laser cutting in the workshop run by the Maker’s Space. This was a light introduction, which gave a sense of familiarity for the tools involved. Though this was not enough to have me feeling confident, I found I was curious about exploring.

When I went to create my ‘Brain Box’ I set a task that was a low entry point. A templated box which helped me build confidence in the programs to use. I utilised that knowledge to then etch my own design.
